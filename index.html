Preamble

(i)Dean Park Primary School Parent Council is the Parent Council established for Dean Park Primary school in Balerno, Edinburgh.

(ii)The Parent Council is established by the school's Parent Forum under section 6 of the Scottish Schools (Parental Involvement) Act 2006.

Part 1
Conduct of Parent Council business

j001Decision making

(1)Decisions of the Parent Council are to be taken as follows.

(2)At a meeting of the Parent Council, the presiding member is to propose a decision and invite the voting members present to indicate whether they agree with it.

(3)If all of the voting members present indicate that they agree with it, the proposed decision is adopted as the decision of the Parent Council.

(4)If any voting member indicates disagreement with the proposed decision, the presiding member must invite the voting members present to vote on it.

(5)In the event of a vote on a proposed decision:

(a)the proposed decision is adopted as the Parent Council's decision if a simple majority of the voting members present agree with it,

(b)if the votes are tied, and the decision in question is not an appointment decision, the presiding member has a casting vote,

(c)if the votes are tied, and the decision in question is an appointment decision, whether or not the proposed decision is adopted as the Parent Council's decision is to be decided in a manner:

(i)that is chosen by the presiding member, and

(ii)which, in the presiding member's opinion, renders the outcome unforeseeable (for example, a coin toss).

(6)In paragraph (5), "appointment decision" means a decision to:

(a)co-opt a person as a member, or

(b)appoint a person as an office holder.

(7)For the avoidance of doubt:

(a)the presiding member may:

(i)indicate disagreement with the proposed decision,

(ii)vote differently for the purposes of sub-paragraphs (a) and (b) of paragraph (5),

(b)a voting member who has withdrawn from the meeting at the time when the decision is to be made is not to be regarded as present at the meeting for the purposes of this article.
